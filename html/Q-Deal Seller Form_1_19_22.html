Description of Sponsor and Guarantor Criteria

This Application is for Multifamily TEBS Sponsor and Guarantor status only. It is not an application for approval as a Single Family Seller/Servicer, Multifamily Conventional Seller/Servicer, Multifamily SBL Seller/Servicer or Multifamily TAH Seller/Servicer.

Additionally, the Sponsor and Guarantor do not need to be approved by Single Family prior to applying for Multifamily approval. Multifamily approval does not imply or include approval for Single Family. Multifamily does not have delegated authority to grant Single Family approval. Therefore, a Multifamily TEBS Sponsor application does not substitute for a Single Family application.

Definitions

Definitions of some key terms used by Freddie Mac are provided below.

  1. - First lien mortgage on real estate on which there is located a structure used for office, retail, wholesale, industry and/or storage.
  2. - Cash mortgages or tax exempt bond credit enhancements, whose purpose is for financing properties subject to rent and/or income restrictions for the purpose of providing housing to low and moderate income persons or persons renting in under-served areas as defined by HUD for Freddie Mac.
  3. - First lien mortgage on real estate on which there is a structure designed principally for residential use by five or more families.
  4. - An institution approved to service mortgages purchased by Freddie Mac.
  5. - An institution approved to sell mortgages to and service mortgages purchased by Freddie Mac.
