Multifamily Conventional Seller/Servicer Form
General Instructions

ONLY those applicants seeking a Conventional Selling and Servicing license need to complete this form.

By completing the information on this form you will facilitate Freddie Mac's review the applicant's conventional production, underwriting and servicing operations. The information you provide, unless otherwise specified, should be as of the date you complete and sign this form.

If the applicant is applying to be a Servicer-Only counterparty, then they will only be required to complete the servicing portion of this form.

For the purpose of this form, servicing staff refers to all full-time multifamily collection, foreclosure, real estate owned (REO), and property inspection staff, as well as support staff including payoff, escrow, and vault/note maintenance personnel.
